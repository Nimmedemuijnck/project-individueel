What is their history?

Climate Cleanup emerged as a beacon of hope amid the looming threat of climate change, founded by its visionary leader, Chami Akmeemana. With a background in software engineering, Akmeemana recognized the urgent need for action and embarked on a mission to harness the power of nature to combat the impending climate crisis.

The inception of Climate Cleanup marked the beginning of an extraordinary journey. Akmeemana's vision led to the establishment of this non-profit organization, dedicated to addressing the climate crisis head-on. Their audacious goal was nothing short of miraculous - to remove a monumental 1500 gigatons of carbon dioxide (CO2) from the atmosphere, a challenge that required innovative solutions.

Nature-based solutions formed the foundation of Climate Cleanup's strategy. They delved into methods such as reforestation and agroforestry, recognizing the untapped potential of natural systems, including forests and wetlands, to capture and store carbon. Their approach was rooted in the understanding that aligning with nature was essential in addressing climate change.

However, Climate Cleanup's mission was far from solitary. They adopted a collaborative approach, forging partnerships with a diverse group of stakeholders, bringing together entrepreneurs, governments, financial institutions, scientists, and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) in a collective effort to develop holistic climate solutions. It was a collective response to an existential threat.

An integral aspect of their work was the meticulous tracking of carbon removal efforts. Climate Cleanup emphasized the importance of accurate carbon accounting and pioneered the development of robust methods and verification systems to ensure transparency and effectiveness in their climate solutions.

They coined the term "NatureTech" to encompass an emerging sector of climate solutions. Climate Cleanup aimed to support and expand businesses in this sector, leveraging technology and innovation to make a substantial impact on climate change. It was the fusion of nature and technology for a sustainable future.

The vision painted by Climate Cleanup depicted a world where humanity and nature coexisted harmoniously. It was a realm where the planet thrived, and people became stewards of restoration, not only for Earth but also for their own species. This vision was more than just a dream; it was their guiding star.

While their historical timeline may not be extensively documented, the mission, approach, and vision of Climate Cleanup remained steadfast. Their goal remained unwavering: to combat climate change through nature-based solutions and to transform the global economy into a regenerative force. Their tireless efforts resonated as a powerful call to action in the face of an urgent climate crisis and illuminated the path toward a more sustainable future.
